Experience & Education

Dec 2025

Introduction to Pre-breeding

Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO)

Completed specialized training on pre-breeding fundamentals and its applications in plant genetic resources. The course covered optimal use of genetic diversity, criteria for implementing pre-breeding programs, and essential plant biology and genetics concepts for successful genetic resource management and crop improvement strategies.

2025

Intern Researcher

Delphy Improvement Center, Bleiswijk (NL)

Curricular internship completed as part of the MSc program, carried out within the FieldLab Vertical Farming project, aimed at optimizing the cultivation of leafy vegetables and culinary herbs. Involvement in the setup and development of experimental trials focused on optimizing greenhouse crops (tomato, cucumber, strawberry, and blueberry) through the implementation of innovative technologies for irrigation, artificial lighting, and pollination. Activities included applied research, crop management, data collection and processing, and statistical analysis.

2023 - 2025

MSc Plant Sciences

Wageningen University & Research (NL)

Specialization in Greenhouse Horticulture, with a focus on plant physiology in PFAL systems (Plant Factories with Artificial Lighting). Analysis and management of climate control strategies aimed at optimizing crop growth and quality in protected environments, both pre- and post-harvest. Master's thesis conducted at the Department of Horticulture and Product Physiology of Wageningen University, investigating the effects of different daytime and nighttime electrical conductivity levels on Thai basil grown in a hydroponic system. Final grade: 8/10.

Customer consultancy on herbal properties, scientific research support for pharmaceutical companies, and management of shipments/invoicing.

BSc Applied Pharmaceutical Sciences

University of Bologna (IT)

Specialization in Herbal Techniques, with in-depth training in the botany of medicinal plants, pharmaceutical chemistry, and natural organic compounds. Study of the main techniques for the extraction, purification, and identification of secondary metabolites from plant tissues.
